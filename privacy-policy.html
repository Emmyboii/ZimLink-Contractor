SMS/Text Messaging Program

Consent and Opt-In

We obtain consent to send text messages through direct verbal communication during our initial phone calls with technicians. When we contact you by phone about work opportunities, we will ask for your explicit consent to send you text messages related to:

  • Work order assignments and updates
  • Availability requests for future jobs
  • Work completion confirmations
  • Service-related communications

If you have previously worked with us and provided consent, we may continue to text you about new opportunities and work-related matters. Your consent covers ongoing communication related to our professional relationship.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your service needs and activity. You may receive up to several messages per week during active work orders, with fewer messages during inactive periods.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of text messages at any time by:

  • Texting "STOP" to any message you receive from us
  • Replying "UNSUBSCRIBE" to any of our text messages
  • Contacting us directly at +1 651-815-3444

You will receive a confirmation message when you opt out. After opting out, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us unless you re-subscribe or we need to send critical service-related communications.
