About Zimlink Contractors

At Zimlink Contractors, we believe that home repairs should be simple, affordable, and done right the first time. Based in Newport, MN, our locally owned and operated company offers trusted handyman services, minor electrical fixes, and minor plumbing repairs tailored to meet the unique needs of homeowners in our community.

Our skilled team handles each project with care, ensuring your home remains safe and comfortable. From fixing that leaky faucet to upgrading light fixtures or tackling general repairs, we bring professionalism and passion to every job.

Customer satisfaction is at the heart of what we do. We listen closely, communicate clearly, and work efficiently to exceed your expectations while respecting your budget and timeline.

We are committed to using quality materials, modern techniques, and maintaining a clean, respectful work environment. Your home is your sanctuary, and we treat it as if it were our own.
